Our Workflow

How We Work

01

Concept

The first and most critical step — understanding your project's requirements, expectations, overall vision, goals, scope, features, purpose, cost and functionality.

02

Design

Rough sketches and drawings that show the basic ideas from the concept phase. Initial research on local compliance and regulations is completed in this stage.

03

Development

We take schematic design results one step further — finalizing the design and specifying all items including materials, finishes and structural systems.
